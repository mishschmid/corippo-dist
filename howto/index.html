House Guide

Everything you need to know about your stay in Corippo

Arrival & Departure

  • Check-in time: 3:00 PM
  • Check-out time: 11:00 AM
  • Please leave the key in the designated lockbox upon departure
  • Ensure all windows are closed and lights are turned off

Household Appliances

Washing Machine

  • Located in the basement
  • Use eco-friendly detergent provided
  • Maximum load: 7 kg
  • Recommended temperature: 30°C for regular wash

Dishwasher

  • Use the provided eco-friendly tablets
  • Please scrape food remains before loading
  • Run only when full

Heating System

  • Thermostat located in the living room
  • Recommended temperature: 20°C
  • Please turn down when leaving the house

Waste Management

  • Separate waste into:
    • Recyclables (paper, glass, plastic)
    • Organic waste
    • General waste
  • Recycling bins are located behind the house
  • Please flatten cardboard boxes
