Ponte dei Salti – The Iconic Stone Bridge

Ponte dei Salti – The Iconic Stone Bridge

Visit the picturesque double-arched stone bridge in Lavertezzo, the most photographed spot in the valley. This medieval bridge spans the crystal-clear Verzasca River and is a popular destination for swimmers and photographers alike.

The bridge dates back to the 17th century and is a masterpiece of medieval engineering. Its reflection in the emerald-green waters below creates one of the most iconic images of the Verzasca Valley.
