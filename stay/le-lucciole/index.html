Le Lucciole

An authentic rustico experience in charming Corippo

About this place

Le Lucciole (The Fireflies) is a charming rustico that embodies the essence of traditional Ticinese living. Nestled in the enchanting village of Corippo, this carefully restored stone house offers a peaceful retreat surrounded by natural beauty and mountain tranquility.

Ideal for couples or small families, Le Lucciole provides a warm and inviting atmosphere where you can disconnect from everyday life and reconnect with nature in one of Switzerland's most beautiful hidden gems.

The space

Bedrooms: Thoughtfully designed sleeping areas with comfortable beds and traditional charm provide a restful night's sleep in the mountain air.

Kitchen: A well-appointed kitchen with modern appliances makes meal preparation easy and enjoyable.

Living area: The welcoming living space features traditional architectural elements that create a cozy and authentic atmosphere.

Outdoor space: Relax in your private outdoor area, perfect for enjoying the peaceful village ambiance and mountain views.

Things to know

Getting there: Corippo is accessible by car. Parking is available in the village. The house is located within the pedestrian area, requiring a short walk with your luggage.

Check-in: Self check-in with a key safe. Detailed directions will be provided upon booking.

Water: The tap water in Corippo is of excellent quality and safe to drink.

Mobile coverage: Mobile phone coverage is available, and the house has reliable WiFi.
