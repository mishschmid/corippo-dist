Casa Cinciarella

A cozy rustico retreat in the heart of Corippo

About this place

Casa Cinciarella is a lovingly restored rustico in the picturesque village of Corippo. This cozy retreat offers an authentic Ticinese experience, blending traditional stone architecture with modern amenities for a comfortable stay in the Swiss mountains.

Perfect for couples or solo travelers seeking peace and natural beauty, Casa Cinciarella provides a serene base for exploring the stunning Verzasca Valley and its many hiking trails.
