Welcome to

Le Lucciole

We are delighted to welcome you to Corippo - one of the most charming villages in Switzerland. To help you enjoy your stay to the full, we have put together some useful information here.

Way to the Rustici

The Le Lucciole houses are located between Corippo Bivio and the village center. It's the only houses on the way to Corippo that exist below the main road. Just park at road and then it's a 2-3 minutes walk downhill to the houses.

You can find the key in the key box next to the front door.

The pink facade is clearly visible

Garbage & Recycling

In Corippo, we separate the following waste:

Glass (disposal between the bridge and the Corippo Bivio bus stop)

PET (disposal behind the church)

Metal (disposal behind the church)

Other waste (disposal behind the church)

Please always use the provided green waste bags for residual waste.

Making Fire You can light a fire in the wood stove using the provided wood. Please be mindful: all wood is flown to the Rustico by helicopter
Afterward, use the black bucket to dispose of the ashes.
If the bucket is full, replace it with the spare bucket in the cellar, just below the sun room.
No Smoking Please do not smoke inside the building - you can easily do this on the balcony or in the garden.

Parking

Baker

A highlight for early risers:

The mobile baker comes to the piazza in Corippo every day (except Sundays) at around 8:15 am. He rings the church bell briefly when he arrives

There is fresh bread, croissants and small treats.

To be sure or to make a reservation, you can contact him by phone (preferably in Italian or French, German & English are also possible).

Delicious breakfast at the Albergo Diffuso

The Albergo Diffuso offers breakfast exclusively for our guests. Bread, plaited bread, butter, homemade jam, honey, yoghurt with granola and fruit, cheese, sausage and eggs in all variations. Drinks (coffee, tea, juice and water) are also included in the price of CHF 35.

Shopping facilities

The nearest shopping facility is the Centro Commerciale in Tenero, around 15 minutes away by car

Alternatively, you can easily get there directly by bus from Corippo Bivio - without changing buses.
